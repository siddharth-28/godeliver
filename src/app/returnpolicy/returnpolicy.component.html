Product Returns, Refund and Replacements

Returns All products purchased on www.godeliver.in can be returned within 7 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused. It should be in the original packaging accompanied with the receipt or proof of purchase.

Please email your request along with the order number to support@godeliver.in and ship the product to: Plot No 29, Road No 76, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana 500033.

After receiving the product our testing team will verify, approve and process the refund.

Refund method

  • After receiving the product, we will inspect it and notify you.
  • We will also communicate the status of your refund after the inspection.
  • After approval, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or original method of payment).
  • If the payment method at the time of purchase is credit card / debit card / wallet or net banking the total order amount you paid is returned to the same mode of payment.
  • If the payment method is cash on delivery (COD), then the total order amount you paid is credited to your www.godeliver.in account.
  • You can use this amount to make any future product purchases on www.godeliver.in We will refund the amount within 7 working days to the original method of payment (except COD).
  • If partial product or product with tampered packaging is returned, then the refund will not be processed. All accessories and items that you received along with the product should be returned together.

Replacements

In case of any issue with the product:
  • Please reach out to us through the contact information provided on the warranty card or click here.
  • While claiming, please share the details of the purchase order.
  • If the product is under warranty, we will arrange for a reverse pickup from the respective address.
  • After receiving the product, our technical team will test the product for defects (if any) and replace the product (if applicable).
